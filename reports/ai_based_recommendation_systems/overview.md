# Recommendation Systems Overview

## Introduction
Sehat Saathi uses a rule-based recommendation system to generate workout and diet plans that are personalized, explainable, and safe.

## Core Approaches
### Rule-Based Decision Making
- Deterministic and easy to audit
- Suitable for health and fitness guidance
- Works well with injury and allergy constraints

### Data-Driven Calculations
- BMI, BMR, TDEE, and target calories
- Macro allocation based on user goals
- Portion scaling for meal planning

## Recommendation Output
- Weekly workout schedule
- Daily meal plan
- Safety confidence score
- Progress feedback and logging support

## Why this approach works
- Clear logic for users and evaluators
- Low risk compared with opaque black-box systems
- Easy to test and explain in a project report
- Fits the Sehat Saathi brand direction of practical health planning

## Conclusion
The Sehat Saathi engine shows how simple recommendation logic can still deliver a polished and useful fitness product when the rules, data, and interface are designed together.
